Event Planning

Is your event a Lyfe Event?

When it comes to planning your special event, Lyfe Events is available to assist from whichever level you need. We offer 3 tiers of event services:

Event Planning Packages

Tier 1: The Just Need Help Package: Day of Event Coordination Only

Includes: Assistance with creating a day-of timeline

  • Running of processional and recessional
  • Decor install/takedown
  • Execution of event timeline
  • Advice on protocol and etiquette
  • Payment/gratuity distribution
  • Gathering of all gifts, valuables, and decor items to be placed into a predetermined vehicle or location
  • Ensuring all rules and regulations are followed at the venue

Tier 2: The Work-Shy Package: Partial Planning Services

Includes: Limited Assistance with event planning

  • Vendor coordination and logistics
  • Prepare detailed day-of timeline
  • Design of floor plans and layouts
  • Event design assistance and implementation
  • Everything included in the Tier 1

Tier 3: The S.O.S Package Full Event Planning Services

Includes: Assistance with all aspects of planning

  • All event logistics
  • Prepare detailed timelines, checklists, and any additional documents
  • RSVP/Invitation management
  • Budget creation/management
  • Venue sourcing
  • Handle all contracts and negotiations
  • Vendor management and coordination
  • Decor design and execution
  • Transportation of items from ceremony to reception
  • Everything included in Tiers 2 & 1
